Drawing Of The Dead


How To Play

WASD to Move the Player. Spacebar to Jump

Q to Move the Camera to the Left and E to move the Camera to the Right

You will have to use your drawing skills to kill Zombies that spawns from the Portals.

Zombies will have a Shape above their head. You will need to draw and match the Shape.

Left Click and move your mouse around to draw on the screen. Right Click after drawing to check your drawings.

If your drawing is recognized, the closest visible Zombie of the shape drawn with be smited.


Some of the drawings works better with your starting point being from the Right side. (e.g The Line Shape)
Check How To Play to see the List of Shapes/Hints
